Name / Acronym

DaaS (Graphics)

↳ Dan as a Service

DaaS (Dan as a Service) is a design and coaching subscription service run by Dan Newman.

Over the past two decades he’s led design teams and worked hands-on at startups like Xero, Axiom, and Stedi; as well as at agencies, designing award-winning work for the likes of the Klim Type Foundry and Te Papa (Museum of New Zealand).

/

Services

Coaching + Advising + Designing

D-01 / D-02 / D-03

Whether you’re in San Francisco or on Stewart Island, get an experienced design partner whenever you need it.

DaaS (Graphics) offers plans that are flexible for individuals, early-stage founders, and go-to-market teams. If you’re after advice, or a new creative direction (branding, logos, swag, conference collateral, or a site), there’s a plan to help.

&

Tagline / Mission

Authenticity & Aesthetics

0% [ █ █ ▄ _ _ ] 100%

Making authentic and aesthetically unique work with people who care deeply about design is the DaaS (Graphics) mission.

With AI rapidly changing how we work, having an authentic voice, personality, and sense of taste is essential for brands that want to stand out and resonate with their audiences. It’s an amazing (and wild) time to be creative.

Experience

Dan Newman (MCGD)

1982 → Present

From coloring competitions in the 80s to making websites (without AI) in the 00s, Dan (^_^)/~ has always been creating.

He also has a Master’s degree (w/ Distinction) in Computer Graphic Design from the Whanganui School of Design (New Zealand), which included a scholarship to study at Nottingham University (England), and FH Oberösterreich (Austria).
