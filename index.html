Name / Acronym

DaaS (Graphics)

↳ Dan as a Service

DaaS (Dan as a Service) is a subscription service for design leadership and branding run by Dan Newman.

Over the past two decades he’s led design teams and worked hands-on at startups like Xero, Axiom, and Stedi; as well as at agencies, designing award-winning work for the likes of the Klim Type Foundry and Te Papa (Museum of New Zealand).

/

Services

Leadership / D-02

Design / D-03

Get design and leadership support for your team, startup, or business — only when you need it.

Whether you’re after a new creative direction, brand, landing pages, pitch decks, swag, conference collateral, or a website redesign, DaaS (Graphics) offers plans that are flexible for startups, early-stage founders, and go-to-market teams.

Tagline / Mission

Authenticity & Aesthetics

Making authentic and aesthetically unique work with people who care deeply about design is the DaaS (Graphics) mission.

With AI rapidly changing how we work, having an authentic voice, personality, and sense of taste is essential for brands that want to stand out and resonate with their audiences. It’s an amazing (and wild) time to be creative.
